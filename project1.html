Introduction

We are interested in making a business website about improving your fortune because Thai people have been worshiping sacred things for a long time and also pay attention to fortune telling, improve your luck, or ask for blessings at places that have been recommended over and over. Not only Thai people, but foreigners are also interested in coming to ask for blessings as well. In addition to paying respects in places, many people are also interested in surfing the web to see predictions. Find auspicious numbers and looking at the predictions from youtube, We thought that the market here is quite large.If we have an online service that can provide complete convenience, it should be a good selling point.

    Problems and Solutions

    Limitations of using Wix

  • The fetish can't display the name of the selling shop and rate the products --> Include the store name and rating in the description of each product
  • Adjusting the number of customers allowed to purchase tours is not possible --> Take this section and further develop it in Figma Link to Figma
  • In the fortune teller part, it is not possible to prevent private communication between users and doctors --> Remove this feature and replace it with a feature for astrology readings based on zodiac signs and birthdates

    • Usability Test

    The text in the content section is difficult to read due to the layout and font design.

    The filters should be sorted by category first, and then alphabetically.

    The tour status should be color-coded for easier visibility.

    The party size should allow purchasing more than 3 tickets per booking.

    There is redundancy in the profile editing section between the settings page and the personal history page.


    The webpage that the user sees.



    The Wix editor page.

    The images placed at the top of each zodiac sign are partially cut off when the website is actually opened.

    Suggestion

  • Adjust the website's background color to a light tone and make the text dark
  • Adjust the layout of some content sections to have appropriate spacing
  • On the product page, there may be additional filters related to astrology, such as products for zodiac signs or different birth dates
  • Add a confirm receipt button
  • Increase the party size for tours to 4-5 people per booking
